Our Reputation. The best of traditional building methods and modern standards.

Warnes Builders are renowned for their quality homes and building work in the Lowestoft area.

If you are looking for a builder to do quality job with attention to detail we have the skills and experience to give you a really successful Job. Warnes Builders have a proven track record in producing quality homes with ‘Warnes Built’ being a major selling point for second hand properties.

Even properties over 60 years old are still selling with the ‘Warnes built’ tag.

Brothers Brian and Douglas, with their sister Ruth, all committed Christians, are carrying on a family tradition in quality building which dates back to their great grandfather. Brian and Douglas are both masters at their own trade and qualified in building management. They examine all systems carefully and choose only building methods and materials which will achieve sound construction with low maintenance.

While Ruth is in charge of the office managing the accounts, Brian and Douglas carry out all the design work, site management, buying and the construction work. They are equally likely to be found at a computer as with their overalls on cutting a roof or laying paths.
